About Us

SU. Dagduteli and Sons

Founded by Shri. Dagdu Bhau Chandwadkar in 1955, Su. Dagduteli and sons is an iconic brand, predominantly representing the traditional spices,herbs and dry fruit market in Nashik . We had its humble beginnings in a retail store in Nashik more than eight decades ago..

A pioneer in the retailing of Dry Fruits, Spices, Ayurvedic herbs, Su.Dagduteli and sons is a brand that has been synonymous with quality & goodwill since inception ,With our focus on quality and service, we have established ourselves on the global stage and today we are well equipped to manage growing manufacturing requirements with state of the art infrastructure and well trained and qualified manpower.
